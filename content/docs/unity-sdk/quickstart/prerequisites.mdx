## Setup Requirements

1. **Unity Project**
   - A new or existing Unity project (version `2022.3.11f1`or higher)

2. **Okto Dashboard Setup**
   - Create an account on the [Okto Dashboard](https://dashboard.okto.tech/login)
   - Get your [Okto credits](https://dashboard.okto.tech/okto-credits) from the dashboard
   - Obtain your [API keys](https://dashboard.okto.tech/api-keys) from the dashboard
   - Enable the specific [chains and tokens](https://dashboard.okto.tech/wallet-controls) you plan to use in your application
   - Optional: Enable [sponsorship](https://dashboard.okto.tech/paymaster) for the desired chains

3. **Google OAuth Configuration**
   - Set up a [Google Console Project](https://console.cloud.google.com) for authentication
   - Create OAuth 2.0 client credentials
   - Follow our guide to [set up Google OAuth](/docs/uniy-sdk/authenticate-users/google-oauth/google-console-setup)

<Callout type="info">
Additional authentication methods like Email OTP and Phone OTP will be available soon. If you are already using Email OTP, Phone OTP from [Okto V1](https://docs.okto.tech/) you may continue to use the same auth in V2. Refer our [Migration Guide](/docs/unity-sdk/v2migration)
</Callout>

<Callout title="Environment Configuration">
Your project will use the **Sandbox** environment by default. For production deployment, please refer to our [Developer Dashboard Documentation](/docs/developer-admin-dashboard) for upgrade instructions.
</Callout>

## Next Steps

Once you've completed these prerequisites, proceed to the [Quickstart Guide](/docs/unity-sdk) to integrate the Okto SDK into your Unity project.

Need help? Check our [troubleshooting guide](/docs/unity-sdk/troubleshooting) or reach out to our support team at `devrel@coindcx.com`.
