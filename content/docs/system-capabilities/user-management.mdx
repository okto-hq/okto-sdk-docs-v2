## User Log

The **User Log** gives you a detailed list of all users who have interacted with your application through Okto. It provides a snapshot of user activity and growth, including the following metrics:

- **Total Users:**  
  View the overall number of users associated with your vendor ID.
  
- **User Growth:**  
  Track the increase in users over the last month.
  
- **New Users:**  
  Monitor newly onboarded users via your vendor ID and track daily increases.

### Features

- **Sorted Listing:**  
  Users are displayed in descending order based on their last login date and time.
  
- **Search Functionality:**  
  Quickly locate specific users by:
  - **User ID**
  - **Wallet Address**
  - **User Login Details**

## User's Order History

The **User's Order History** section provides a detailed list of all orders placed by users through your application on Okto. This section allows you to review each order's details and status, facilitating better tracking and analysis.

### Features

- **Ordered Display:**  
  Orders are listed in descending order based on their completion datetime.
  
- **Search Functionality:**  
  Easily find specific orders by searching for:
  - **User ID**
  - **Order ID**
  - **Bulk Order ID**
  - **Transaction Hash**

### Filtering Options

To enhance your review and analysis, you can filter the order history by various criteria:
- **Order Type**
- **Network**
- **Status**
